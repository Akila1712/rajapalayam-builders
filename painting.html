Painting Work

Painting services by Rajapalayam Construction include both interior and exterior projects, ensuring long-lasting and attractive results:

Surface Preparation

Thoroughly prepares surfaces by scraping, sanding, and priming to ensure smooth and even paint application.

Interior Painting

Application of washable, anti-fungal, and eco-friendly paints with techniques like textured painting, stenciling, and wall art.

Exterior Painting

Applies durable, weather-resistant paints that protect surfaces from harsh environmental conditions while enhancing curb appeal.

Wood & Metal Finishing

Provides polishing, varnishing, and rust-proof coatings for doors, windows, grills, and metal fittings.

Finishing & Cleanup

Attention to detail to avoid paint splashes, ensuring a neat and professional outcome.
